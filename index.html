REGISTRATIONS

"If you are locked in a room with books for two years and throw away the key, you can probably make it into DA-IICT".

Turns out, you can pretty much do the same once you're in DA-IICT too. Except now, you can leave the books inside, and lock yourself out of the room. Because such is the variety and vibrancy of extracurricular life in DA-IICT, you're destined to find worthwile and meaningful activities to pursue for every year you spend in this campus. And DA-IICT Sports is one of the more brilliant facets of this life.

We possess a good sports infrastructure which includes Sports Activity Center(SAC), Football and Cricket grounds, volleball and basketball courts.

But more importantly, we cater to all degrees of enthusiasm too. From the most intensive training camps for Inter-college sports, to daily recreational football to playing old-school Sitoliya during specially organised Sports Fests. We, quite honestly, have something for everyone. Or you could live your IPL/EPL dreams by playing in our semester long leagues. The possibilities are endless.

So come, re-live the years spent on school playgrounds and experience the joy of newfound and like-minded friends, the fervour of cheering for your wings, the ultimate glory of a DA-IICT medal.

It all begins here, at DA-IICT Sports.
